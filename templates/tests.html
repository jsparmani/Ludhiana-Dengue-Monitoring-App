Recommended Tests

Tests for Dengue
ELISA based tests are recommended for confirmation of Dengue
NS1 Antigen ELISA
o This is the recommended test when fever is of 5 days (i.e. first 5 days of illness) and IgM Mac ELISA o This is the recommended test when fever is of more than 5 days (i.e. after 5th day of illness)
a. These tests are done free of cost in each district at district level.
b.Rapid tests for dengue are not recommended for dengue as sensitivity of these tests is not reliable
