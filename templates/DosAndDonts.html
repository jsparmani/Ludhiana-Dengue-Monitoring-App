Do's

● Keep body temperature below 39° C. Give the patient paracetamol(not more than 4 time in 24 hours)
● Give large amounts of fluids (water, soup, milk, juice) along with the patient's normal diet
● The patient should take complete rest.
● Cold water sponging in case of high
● Immediately consult a doctor if any of the following manifestations appear
         a.   Red spots or points on the skin
         b.   Bleeding from the nose or gums frequent vomiting
         c.   Vomiting with blood
         d.   Sleepiness
         e.   Constant crying
         f.   Abdominal pain
         g.   Excessive thirst(dry mouth)
         h.   Pale, cold and clammy skin
         i.   Difficulty in breathing

Don'ts

● Do not wait in case the above symptoms appear
● Immediately consult a doctor, it is crucial to quickly get treatment in case of these complications
● Do not take Aspirin, Brufen or Ibubrufen
