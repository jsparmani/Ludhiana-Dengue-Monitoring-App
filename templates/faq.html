FAQs

What is Dengue?
Dengue is a viral fever.

How is dengue transmitted?
Dengue is transmitted after the bite of an infected mosquito

What is the mosquito responsible for dengue?
Aedes aegypti is the mosquito responsible for transmission of dengue to human beings

How does Aedes mosquito look like?
Aedes has white and black stripes on legs, body and wings and is known as Tiger Mosquito
