Basic Questions

Q1) What are the breeding sites of Aedes Mosquito?
Ans1. Aedes breeds in artificial collection of clean stagnant water within and around houses. Aedes mosquitoes are container breeder viz., cement tanks, overhead tanks, underground tanks, tyres, desert coolers, pitchers, discarded containers, jack ma terials, potholes, rooftop, window parapet, ornamental fountain, lucky bamboo pot, money plant bottle etc, in which water stagnates for more than a week.


Q2) Does the Aedes breed in dirty polluted water & drains?
Ans2. No, they breed only in clean water.


Q3) How long eggs of Aedes takes to become adult Mosquito?

Q4) What is the average life span of an adult Aedes?
Ans4. The life span for adult mosquitoes is around three weeks


Q5) What is the identification of larvae of Aedes?
Ans5. Larvae of Aedes are found in clean water collections. They are highly motile and they come to surface of water for oxygen.


Q6) What is the Biting pattern of Aedes mosquitos?
Ans6. Aedes bites during day time.


Q7) When does Dengue vector bite?
Ans7. Dengue vector mosquitoes bite during day time usually in morning (8-10 am) & afternoon (3-5 pm) hours.


Q8) What are the resting sites of Aedes mosquito.?
Ans7. Cool shady corners of the rooms like under the furniture (table, chair etc), behind curtains, paintings, hangings & almirahs
